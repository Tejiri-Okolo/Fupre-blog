About Student Blogger: FUPRE Chapter

This blog is dedicated to students all over Nigeria who enjoy and content creation. This platform was designed to encourage students to take interest in both writing and the operations of the university. From how the school is run, to how the lecturers and students interact. The infrastructure and quality of education that is being provided and how much they have gained since getting admitted into the school. This blog is also to encourage students to voice their concerns on the activities surrounding the university. Students should be allowed to express their opinions about their experiences during Lectures, Lab classes, Field trips and Examinations. The blog is designed to support the SUG and present them with growing issues that students face. Students may also share personal information about their aspirations or experience to encourage incoming aspirants and lower level students. This platform only exists to promote positivity, encourage growth and build unity amongst the university. Abusing and Tarnishing the reputation of individuals is strictly forbidden and discouraged. As well as posting false or offensive information about the school, staffs or students. Each Posted article is reviewed before they are posted on the blog. Do well to build a positive and fun community amongst your fellow writers.
